# Super Mario - 3 Level Adventure 🍄

A browser-based platformer game inspired by Super Mario Bros with 3 unique levels!

## 🎮 Controls

| Action | Keys |
|--------|------|
| Move Left | ← |
| Move Right | → |
| Jump | ↑ or Spacebar |
| Restart Game | R |
| Continue to Next Level | Space (after level complete) |

## 🏆 Game Features

### 3 Unique Levels
1. **Level 1 - Grass Plains** 🌿
   - Classic grassy terrain with question blocks and goombas
   - Gentle introduction to gameplay mechanics

2. **Level 2 - Underground Caves** 🕳️
   - Darker, underground setting with platforms
   - Tricky jumps and hidden passages

3. **Level 3 - Castle of Doom** 🏰
   - Challenging castle-themed level
   - Lava pits, hard blocks, and more enemies

### Game Mechanics
- **Jump Physics**: Realistic gravity and momentum
- **Enemy Stomping**: Jump on goombas to defeat them
- **Question Blocks**: Hit from below to reveal coins (+50 points)
- **Breaking Bricks**: Stomp to break standard brick blocks (+10 points)
- **Coin Collection**: Grab coins floating in the air (+10 points each)
- **Goal**: Reach the flag at the end of each level
- **Lives System**: Start with 3 lives, respawn after falling or enemy collision
- **Scoring**: Collect coins, stomp enemies, and break blocks for points

### Visuals
- **Textured Graphics**: Custom-drawn sprites for Mario, enemies, tiles, and items
- **Level-Specific Themes**: Different color palettes for each level
- **Animations**: Running, jumping, and enemy movement animations
- **Particle Effects**: Brick shattering and enemy defeat effects

## 📊 Scoring

| Action | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Coin Collection | +10 |
| Question Block Hit | +50 |
| Enemy Stomp | +100 |
| Brick Break | +10 |

## 🎯 How to Play

1. **Navigate** left and right across the level
2. **Jump** over obstacles and onto platforms
3. **Stomp** goombas (brown mushroom enemies) by landing on their heads
4. **Collect** coins and hit question blocks for bonus points
5. **Reach** the red flag at the end to complete the level
6. **Survive** all 3 levels to win!

## 💡 Tips

- Hold the jump button while in the air for a small boost
- Time your jumps carefully on moving platforms
- Stomp enemies to clear your path
- Breaking blocks can reveal hidden paths or items
- The final level is the hardest - tread carefully!

## 🚀 Getting Started

Simply open `mario.html` in your web browser to play!

**Note**: For the best experience, use a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari).
